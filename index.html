About Me

Jason Smith is a Master’s candidate in Management Studies at the Robert H. Smith School of Business, University of Maryland. With a strong foundation in business analytics and strategy, he is passionate about using data to drive decision-making and solve complex challenges.

His recent capstone consulting project involved representing the Smith School as lead consultant for ServiceNow's federal NASA account. In this role, he developed strategic recommendations aimed at increasing product usage and client engagement across the NASA enterprise.

Jason is currently building a portfolio of Power BI dashboards and interactive business intelligence projects to further demonstrate his growing expertise in data analytics. These works will soon be available on this site.

This website serves as a professional showcase of Jason’s academic achievements, consulting experience, and technical skills in business management and analytics.
